Newly built FSRUs are purpose-designed vessels constructed from scratch for efficient LNG storage and regasification. In contrast, converted FSRUs are existing ships, often LNG carriers, retrofitted to perform regasification functions. Both types play a crucial role in the LNG supply chain, offering flexible solutions for countries seeking to enhance energy security and reduce dependence on traditional pipelines.</p></p>
