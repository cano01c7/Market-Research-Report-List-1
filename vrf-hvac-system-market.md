<p><p>The VRF (Variable Refrigerant Flow) HVAC system market includes two main types of branch piping: horizontal and vertical. Horizontal branch pipes are utilized in low-rise buildings, allowing efficient refrigerant distribution across multiple indoor units on the same floor. In contrast, vertical branch pipes serve high-rise structures, facilitating vertical refrigerant flow between floors. Both systems enhance energy efficiency and provide flexible zoning options, catering to diverse architectural designs and optimizing climate control in commercial and residential applications.</p></p>
