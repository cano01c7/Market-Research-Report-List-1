<p><ul><li>DOT 3</li><li>DOT 4</li><li>DOT 5</li><li>DOT 5.1</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The brake and clutch fluids market encompasses various types, notably DOT 3, DOT 4, DOT 5, and DOT 5.1. DOT 3 is glycol-based and commonly used in older vehicles, offering good performance at moderate temperatures. DOT 4 also uses glycol but has a higher boiling point, making it suitable for newer vehicles. DOT 5 is silicon-based and compatible with synthetic materials, while DOT 5.1, also glycol-based, offers high performance and is engineered for advanced brake systems. Each type meets specific vehicle requirements and performance standards.</p></p>
