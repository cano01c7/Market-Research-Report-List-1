<p><ul><li>Direct Draw System</li><li>Air Cooled System</li><li>Glycol Cooled System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The beer dispense systems market features several types. A Direct Draw System uses shorter beer lines to connect kegs directly to taps, ideal for small venues. Air Cooled Systems rely on ambient air to maintain beer temperature, typically found in bars with limited space. Glycol Cooled Systems circulate a glycol-water mixture through insulated lines, ensuring precision temperature control over longer distances, suited for larger operations. Each system caters to different venue sizes and operational needs, enhancing beer service quality.</p></p>
