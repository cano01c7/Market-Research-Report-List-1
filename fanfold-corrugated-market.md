<p><p>The fanfold corrugated market is categorized into single-walled and double-walled types. Single-walled corrugated materials consist of one layer of fluting sandwiched between two liners, providing lightweight and moderate strength, ideal for lightweight shipping needs. Double-walled corrugated materials feature two layers of fluting and three liners, offering enhanced durability and protection for heavier or fragile goods. The choice between these types depends on the packaging requirements, with single-walled suited for everyday items and double-walled for more demanding applications.</p></p>
