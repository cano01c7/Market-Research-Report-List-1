<p><p>The Satellite Automatic Identification System (AIS) market primarily features two types of transponders: Class A and Class B. Class A transponders are mandatory for large commercial vessels, providing precise location and navigation data to enhance maritime safety and traffic monitoring. In contrast, Class B transponders are intended for smaller vessels and recreational boats, offering simplified tracking and communication features at a lower cost. Both transponder types facilitate real-time maritime situational awareness, supporting improved maritime operations and regulatory compliance.</p></p>
