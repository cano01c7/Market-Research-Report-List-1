<p><p>The articulated welding robot market comprises two main types: spot welding robots and arc welding robots. Spot welding robots are designed for precise, localized welding tasks, primarily used for joining sheet metal in automotive manufacturing. They excel in high-speed operations and efficiency. In contrast, arc welding robots utilize a continuous arc to melt and fuse materials, suitable for various applications, including heavy fabrication and construction. Both types enhance productivity, consistency, and safety in welding processes across industries.</p></p>
