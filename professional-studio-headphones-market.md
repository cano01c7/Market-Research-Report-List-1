<p><p>The professional studio headphones market can be categorized into three main types: closed-back, semi-open back, and fully-open back. Closed-back headphones provide excellent noise isolation, making them ideal for recording environments. Semi-open back headphones offer a balance between isolation and a natural sound stage, suitable for mixing. Fully-open back headphones deliver an expansive soundstage and a more natural listening experience, favored for critical listening in controlled environments. Each type serves distinct needs based on sound isolation and auditory experience.</p></p>
