<p><p>The clutch friction plates market features two primary types: single side and double side plates. Single side friction plates are designed for applications where torque transfer is required from one side only, making them suitable for compact configurations. In contrast, double side friction plates deliver enhanced performance by allowing torque transfer from both sides, resulting in greater durability and efficiency. Both types cater to various automotive and industrial applications, influencing their selection based on performance requirements and space constraints.</p></p>
