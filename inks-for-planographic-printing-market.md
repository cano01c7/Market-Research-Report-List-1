<p><p>Inks for planographic printing are categorized into three main types: water-based inks, solvent-based inks, and UV-cured inks. Water-based inks use water as a solvent, offering eco-friendly options with lower VOC emissions. Solvent-based inks contain organic solvents, providing quick drying and vibrant colors, though with higher VOC levels. UV-cured inks utilize ultraviolet light to instantly dry and cure the ink, enabling high-speed printing and excellent durability, making them ideal for specialty applications and various substrates.</p></p>
