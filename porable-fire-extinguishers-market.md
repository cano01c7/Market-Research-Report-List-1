<p><p>The portable fire extinguishers market includes various types, each designed for specific fire classes. Dry chemical extinguishers utilize powdered agents to combat flammable solids, liquids, and gases. Foam extinguishers suppress fires by forming a barrier, particularly effective against flammable liquids. Carbon dioxide extinguishers are suitable for electrical and flammable liquid fires, displacing oxygen to extinguish flames. Water extinguishers are primarily used for Class A fires involving solids like wood. "Others" may include specialized extinguishers like wet chemical or clean agent types.</p></p>
