<p><p>The Chemical Mechanical Polishing (CMP) pads market is divided into two main types: hard CMP pads and soft CMP pads. Hard CMP pads are utilized for polishing materials that require more substantial pressure and offer greater durability, making them suitable for processes involving harder substrates. In contrast, soft CMP pads are designed for delicate polishing tasks, providing gentler contact to minimize scratching and defects on softer materials. Both types play critical roles in semiconductor manufacturing and other applications requiring precise surface finishes.</p></p>
